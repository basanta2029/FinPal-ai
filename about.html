FinPal: Investing Made Simple

We’re here to make investing feel less intimidating and more empowering — especially for Gen Z. No jargon. No judgment. Just clear, relatable learning that actually makes sense.

Why We Built FinPal

Most investing platforms assume you already get the basics. We didn’t. We were tired of apps that felt overwhelming and confusing — like they skipped the intro chapter.

So we built FinPal: a beginner-friendly space where learning how to invest feels more like chatting with a friend than reading a textbook.

Where We're Headed

This is just our MVP. We're building toward something bigger:

• Personalized learning paths for stocks, crypto, and long-term wealth
• Daily streaks and milestone rewards (yep, like Duolingo)
• Simulated investing games to boost confidence before real trades
• Progress tracking that adapts to how *you* learn

FAQs

  • Is FinPal a trading app?
    Nope. We’re not here to help you trade. We’re here to help you understand what you’re doing first.
  • Do I need any investing experience?
    None. We start with the absolute basics.
  • Will it stay free?
    Yes. The core learning experience will always be free for beginners.
